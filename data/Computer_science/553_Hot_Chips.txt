Hot Chips is technological symposium held every year in August on Stanford University campus. Operating since 1989, it is co-sponsored by IEEE and ACM SIGARCH. The general emphasis of the conference are microprocessors and integrated circuits. Processor makers often announce details about future processor designs at the symposium.
