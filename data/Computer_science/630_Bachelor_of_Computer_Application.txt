Bachelor of Computer Applications is a three-year undergraduate degree course in the field of computer applications/computer science.After BCA the students can do further studies as MCA master in computer application. It is a common degree for CS/IT in Indian universities and is an alternative to the engineering counterpart, BE/BTech in Computer Science/IT which takes 4 years. It is a technical degree that prepares students for a career in the field of computer applications and software development.
