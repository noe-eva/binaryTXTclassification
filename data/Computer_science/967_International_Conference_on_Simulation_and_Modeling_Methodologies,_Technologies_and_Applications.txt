SIMULTECH, the International Conference on Simulation and Modeling Methodologies, Technologies and Applications, is a research conference sponsored by the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) held every other year. [1] It is considered an important conference in system simulation in Europe. The acceptance rate for SIMULTECH has been around 25%. [2]
Like many ACM conferences, SIMULTECH has tutorial talks, technical sessions, and poster sessions. The conference is usually spread over three days. The conference proceedings is indexed by Thomson Reuters [3] and Springer. [4]
