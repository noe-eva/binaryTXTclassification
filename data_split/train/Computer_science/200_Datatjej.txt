Datatjej (translated: "computer girl" or "data girl") is an annual conference for women studying Computer science in Sweden. (The Computer Science education must be at least 4 years long). The conference lasts for 4 days and has about 100 visitors each year. Every year there is a different theme, examples of previous themes are mentorship, back to the future, personal development.
