The IEEE Southwest Symposium on Image Analysis and Interpretation is the IEEE biennial conference on image analysis, computer vision and pattern recognition. [1] [2] It is considered, together with CVPR, the major conference in interpretation of images and video in the United States. [3] It was first held in San Antonio, TX in April, 1996. It is indexed by IEEE [4] and the Institute for Scientific Information (ISI). [5] It is also included in Scopus and Scimago. [6]
